What is 3D design?

3D design means creating objects with width, height, and depth inside a computer — just like real life. Designers build characters, buildings, and entire worlds from scratch. It is used in games, movies, and school projects.

Blender 3D & renders

Blender is 100% free software for building 3D objects, adding textures, and producing a final render — an image that looks like a real photo. Used by Netflix studios, and perfect for Grade 6 beginners.

Moon Animator — Roblox animation plugin

Moon Animator is a free plugin inside Roblox Studio. Animate any character by placing keyframes on a timeline — each keyframe is a pose snapshot. Moon Animator automatically fills in smooth movement between poses using a technique called tweening. No coding needed — just drag, pose, and play.

Cool facts about 3D design & animation
Blender is completely free and is used by studios that produce Netflix original shows.
Moon Animator was built by an independent Roblox developer — not by Roblox itself.
A smooth walk cycle animation typically needs around 8 keyframes to look natural.
Toy Story (1995) was the world's first fully 3D animated film — made before most of us were born.
Some Roblox developers earn real money from games they built while still in middle school.
